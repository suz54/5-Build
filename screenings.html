Film Screenings

Showcasing independent films from emerging creators.

Types of Films

We feature a variety of films, from documentaries to playwrights Of course, we also include the most common genres. Some of the most popular ones are listed below.

  1. Action
  2. Comedy
  3. Drama
  4. Thriller & Horror
  5. Adventure
  6. Romance

When and Where

We have weekly screenings at our main building, in addition, we periodically hold drive-thru screenings down the block in an open lot.
